Rivy

About Rivy

The one stop app to making travel easy. When traveling it can be frustrating to find activities that are structured to your personal interest. Travelling with a family isn't always the easiest, it can be frustrating looking through lists of activities that are structured to accommodating the entire family, without prior planning.

Design Roles

UI/UX Design, Visual Design, UX Researcher

Deliverables

User Survey's, Persona's, User Journey, Wireframes, User Testing

The Problem & The Goal

The Problem Users need a more efficient way to find activities to do on vacation rather than scroll through long list that are not accustomed to their wants and needs.  The Goal Rivy hopes to streamline the process of finding and booking activities using our user research and implementing features to help our users.
